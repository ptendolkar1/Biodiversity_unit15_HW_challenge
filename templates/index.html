What's there in your belly button?

- by Prakash Tendolkar, University of Texas at Austin>

PROJECT GOAL: To make people aware of the types of germs residing in human belly buttons, so that they would keep it clean.
NOTE 1 : Each sample (BB_XXX) contains different types of germs (called 'OTU ID's) from one person's belly button.
NOTE 2 : Each types of germ (OTU ID) is identified by a unique number (e.g. 922, 2389, etc. See pie chart.)
TAKE AWAY: Wash your belly button frequently. (For guidance, refer to Belly button washing frequency dial / gauge below.)
